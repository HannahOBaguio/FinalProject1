Based on the information given by the video, the ocean has a major influence in our planet. Providing oxygen for us to breathe and being able to absorb carbon dioxe. It also provides a home for particular species.

We have a day in June 8, called "World Ocean Day", where we as community learn and spread awareness about the importance of our ocean by doing activities that help conserve aquatic biomes.

Approximately, every single year 19-23 millions of tonnes of plastic are thrown into the aquatic places. Including oceans, rivers, lakes, etc. Causing plastic pollution in our bodies of water.

By providing awareness, education, and solutions━━━We could help prevent the aquatic areas to be destroyed and let our planet Earth thrive once more using the ocean as continuing the growth of life.


The ocean carries various types of ecosystems that became a habitat for particular species.

Examples of Ecosystems that are found in the oceans are the following:

  1. Coral Reefs🪸
  2. Mangroves🌳🪱💧
  3. Rocky Shores🏖️🪨
  4. Open Ocean🌊
  5. Deep-sea🪼
  6. Abyssal Plain🪱
  7. Kelp Forests🌿
  8. Seagrass Meadows෴
  9. Polar Oceans❄️🌊

There are also endagered species living in their respective oceans. Endagered species are animals that are in a serious risk for extinction.

Exaples of endagered animals include:

  • Blue Whales🐋
